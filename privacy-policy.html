Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Email Organizer App ("App") collects, uses, and protects user data. The App is designed only to help users organize and manage their emails.

Information We Access

With your explicit permission, the App accesses limited email data using the Gmail API. This includes:

  • Email metadata such as sender, subject, and date
  • Email content when required for categorization
  • Email labels to organize or modify emails

The App does not send emails on your behalf and does not permanently delete emails without user action.

How We Use Your Data

  • To categorize and organize emails
  • To help users clean and manage inboxes efficiently
  • To improve app performance and reliability

Data Storage

Email data is processed securely and is not stored outside the user's device or Google's services unless required for app functionality. We do not store email content on our own servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data is accessed only to provide the core email organization features.

Data Security

We follow industry-standard security practices to protect user information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

User Control

  • You can revoke Gmail access at any time from your Google Account
  • You can stop using the app at any time
